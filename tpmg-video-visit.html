Designing and implementing The Permanente Medical Groupsâ€™s Video Visits Responsive Web Application.

The TPMG (The Permanente Medical Group) Video Visits responsive web application was created to provide Kaiser Permanente's Members with the ability to attend doctor appointments from the convenience of their mobile devices (iOS or Android). Due to costs, speed, security and compliance issues, the native application that make the actual video call is used only for the call itself. All the scheduling and handshaking is handled by the web application, which in the end (once time for the visit) makes the call and triggers the native app.
