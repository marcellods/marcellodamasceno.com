Check your invite email

To join a video visit as a patient’s guest, now that you’ve installed the mobile app, please go to the email inviting you to the visit and click on the link called “join the video visit here”. That will take you to the private login area where you can join the video visit that you have been invited to.

KP Single Sign On needed

To join a video visit as a clinician, you must be connected to a KP network via wi-fi or VPN and sign on using your NUID.
